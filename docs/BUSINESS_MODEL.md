# Semut LLC Business Model

Version 0.1

This document describes how Semut LLC creates value, generates revenue, and grows over time. Unlike VISION.md, which explains why Semut exists, this document explains how Semut operates as a sustainable business.

⸻

## Mission

Semut LLC builds practical software that helps people coordinate work more effectively.

We believe AI should reduce repetitive work while preserving human judgment.

Rather than replacing people or existing software, Semut composes tools into simpler workflows.

⸻

## Principles

Low Barrier to Entry

Technology should become easier to use, not more complicated.

Semut lowers barriers for:

* Small business owners
* Developers
* Contributors
* Communities

Natural language becomes the primary interface.

⸻

Human in Control

AI assists.

Humans decide.

Business owners always review customer-facing communication before it is sent.

⸻

## Open Development

Semut is developed openly whenever practical.

Open development enables:

* Transparency
* Faster iteration
* Community contributions
* AI-assisted software development
* Long-term trust

We believe execution, customer relationships, and continuous improvement create stronger advantages than closed source alone.

⸻

## Why Customers Pay

Customers are not paying for AI alone.

Customers pay because Semut helps complete business workflows faster while maintaining quality.

The goal is to reduce administrative work so owners can spend more time serving customers.

## Open Source Ecosystem

Semut welcomes contributors.

The objective is not simply to publish code.

The objective is to create an ecosystem where:

* Humans collaborate
* AI assists development
* Contributors learn
* Software improves continuously

Participation is based on contribution quality rather than experience level.
