# Semut Trace Vision

## Purpose

Semut Trace exists to preserve real-world interactions as traceable events.

Most activities in businesses, communities, and organizations leave behind fragments of information spread across emails, messages, reviews, invoices, schedules, documents, and personal notes. While these systems record pieces of activity, the overall history of coordination is often fragmented and difficult to understand.

Semut Trace seeks to provide a common foundation where interactions can be recorded, connected, and preserved as traces.

## Core Principle

Before knowledge can exist, actions must occur.

Every conversation, decision, task, transaction, and collaboration begins as an interaction between people, systems, or environments. These interactions form traces that can be recorded and connected over time.

Semut Trace focuses on preserving these traces before they are summarized, interpreted, or forgotten.

## Layer Perspective

Semut Trace implements the Trace layer of the broader Semut Layer Model.

```text
Trace
  ↓
Memory
  ↓
Knowledge
  ↓
Norm
  ↓
Institution
```

A trace is a recorded event.

As traces accumulate, they form memory.

As memory is analyzed, knowledge emerges.

As knowledge becomes repeated and shared, norms emerge.

As norms persist across time and participants, institutions emerge.

## Product Philosophy

Semut believes that successful products emerge from a continuous feedback loop between customers, contributors, and AI.

Rather than being purely customer-driven or purely vision-driven, Semut follows a feedback-driven approach.

Customers reveal real problems through their daily work and interactions. These become traces that are recorded and observed over time. Contributors and AI analyze these traces, identify recurring patterns, and propose new solutions. Customers then validate those solutions through real usage, creating the next generation of feedback.

```text
Customer Actions
        ↓
      Trace
        ↓
Memory & Knowledge
        ↓
Proposed Solution
        ↓
Customer Validation
        ↓
Repeat
```


In this model, customers are not expected to design products, nor are products built solely from the founder’s vision. Instead, Semut combines observation, experimentation, and rapid iteration to continuously improve its products.

We believe that the best software is created by understanding real work, proposing thoughtful solutions, and learning quickly from reality.

## Long-Term Vision

The long-term vision of Semut Trace is to create a durable record of real-world coordination.

Rather than beginning with assumptions, categories, or predefined structures, Semut Trace begins with observable events and allows higher-order patterns to emerge from recorded activity.

By preserving traces, individuals, businesses, and communities can better understand how coordination forms, evolves, and adapts over time.

## Repository Scope

This repository focuses on the Trace layer.

Its purpose is to collect, store, organize, and expose traceable events that may later contribute to memory, knowledge, norms, and institutions within the broader Semut ecosystem.
